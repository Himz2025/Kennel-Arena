Welcome to Kennel Arena Dog Boarding Center

At Kennel Arena, we believe that every dog deserves love, comfort,
and care even when their owners are away.
Our mission is to provide a safe, fun,
and homely environment
where your furry family members can relax,
play, and socialize while receiving
the highest level of personalized attention.

Whether you need daytime care while you’re at work or a comfortable overnight stay during your travels, our dedicated team ensures your dog’s happiness and well-being come first. With spacious play areas, cozy resting spaces, and plenty of tail-wagging activities, Kennel Arena feels just like a second home.

At Kennel Arena Dog Boarding Center, your dog isn’t just a guest — they’re part of our family.
Come visit us, meet our friendly team, and discover a place where tails never stop wagging.
